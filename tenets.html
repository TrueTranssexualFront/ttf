Tenets

We generally hold these tenets to be true. If you have any feedback, you can talk about it on our subreddit.

  1. Every transsexual man, woman, and child deserves the right to socially and medically transition.
  2. Every transsexual man, woman, and child deserves the right to live as their neurological sex.
  3. Transsexual minors deserve the right to transition, socially and medically, to alleviate dysphoria. If a minor exhibits symptoms of transsexualism, then they should be informed about it and then they should go through therapy to see if transitioning is right for them.
